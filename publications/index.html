LSE Cities Next Urban Economy Series

The Next Urban Economy Series analyses the economic progress and transformation of four cities – Munich, Torino, Barcelona and Seoul – in the context of strong metropolitan growth and the promotion of innovative approaches to urban and regional development policy. Taken together, these city profiles provide city leaders, policymakers and practitioners with valuable resources as they respond to the challenges posed by the current global economic recession and develop their own next urban economy.
